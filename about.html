I am a creative problem-solver. I love learning, and am always looking to enhance my skills and learn new ones.

Making games is something that I am really excited about, and I'm an active member of the Louisville game development scene. One of my largest goals for the community is to serve as a mentor to young people, in particular women, to show that you can turn your dreams into a viable career.

Much of my background is designing with a marketing background, with experience in branding and rebranding, advertising, social media marketing and management, tradeshow planning and event planning.
